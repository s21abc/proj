Our Story

Magnaga Waters Beach Resort is the perfect place to have fun with your family and friends. Established in 1998, Magnaga Waters Beach Resort is a 12-hectare beachfront property in Magnaga, Pantukan, Davao de Oro, Philippines. The facilities include 27 rooms to accommodate 100 guests, beach cottages, two restaurants, event spaces, water slides, and a 25-meter infinity pool.

Our Commitment

At Beach Paradise, we are committed to sustainability and preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings. We actively participate in beach clean-up initiatives and support local conservation efforts to protect marine life and ecosystems.

Meet the Team

Our team is comprised of passionate individuals dedicated to providing exceptional service and ensuring that every guest has a memorable stay. From our front desk staff to our chefs and activity guides, we strive to exceed expectations and create unforgettable experiences.
